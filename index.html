What is the CEO Cohort?

The CEO Cohort is the Tier 1 Institution for tech founders to make money. Our founders include bootstrapped to public company CEOs, seed stage to Series D ventures, from every industry and sector. You should apply if you want to 1000x your business and life.

If you’re accepted to The CEO Cohort, enrollment starts at $5,000 for a full month, and includes everything from Fortune 100 Masterclasses to Tier 1 Fundraising to Live Cohort Coaching Calls to 24/7 ongoing async support. If accelerators take you from 0 to 1, The CEO Cohort takes you from 1 to n.

Every founder works directly with The CEO Cohort General Partner, Jonathan Herzog. Jonathan is a successful ed-tech startup founder, has advised thousands of startups, including coach to OpenAI, Reddit, Coinbase, Flexport, and works closely with a small group of startups he personally hand-selects every batch.

Trained by Bill Campbell, coach to Steve Jobs, Jeff Bezos, and Bill Gates, Jonathan’s work has received endorsements from the likes of Elon Musk, Jack Dorsey, Matt Mullenweg, and Vitalik Buterin. He is Harvard’s Valedictorian, former Goldman Sachs, and built an exit to community of $293+ Billion.

The CEO Cohort takes founders from heads-down product mode to scaled-up distribution mode through the Customer Financed Acquisition Protocol™. It’s the programmatic unlock for Founder Mode.

Today, the CEO Cohort alumni community is one of the most powerful communities in the world, and its members have a strong commitment to help one another. And you might be our next founder. If you want to join us, please do apply!
